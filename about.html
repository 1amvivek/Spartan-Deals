This is Fashionista
YOUR ONE-STOP FASHION DESTINATION

Fashionista is your free, daily shopping update for the clothing, shoes and accessories you love. Just tell us your favorite designers and brands, and we do the legwork, searching hundreds of online stores like Nordstrom, Neiman Marcus, J. Crew, Piperlime and Shopbop, to find your favorite items on sale in your size. We deliver your sales to you when and where you want: on the Web, in email or on your iPhone.Fashionista makes it convenient to get all the things you want on sale in your size.

Services Salemail, Fashionista's flagship product, is an email that alerts you when your favorite brands go on sale in your size. Not only do you get to choose all the brands you love and your personal sizes, but you can pick how frequently you receive these emails (we recommend twice a week!). You can also log onto our site at any time and see your latest sales and "heart" the styles you want to save for later.

Technology Though some people think of us as a fashion site, Fashionista is a robust platform that leverages leading-edge technology to monitor websites, categorize information and match you with items you care about. We work hand-in-hand with more than 150 leading online retailers, have more than four million members and make more than two billion product recommendations each month.

Press Fashionista was recently cited by Time as one of the "50 Best Websites of 2012." We've also been called a site that "revolutionized shopping" (Lucky, December 2010) and one that is "changing the way we shop" (Mashable, 2010). Visit our Featured Press page for more.
